“A Global Sense of Place” Companion Cards

Activity Design, Systems Thinking

A set of letterpressed cards to accompany Doreen Massey’s seminal essay “A Global Sense of Place.”

The cards illustrate the Masseyan concept of “power geometries:” power in relation to flow and movement on a global scale. There are three different cards in the set, for three social groups Massey discusses in the article. Some cards cannot be connected with others. This is to illustrate the difficulty the social group those cards represent might have accessing that other group's space.
