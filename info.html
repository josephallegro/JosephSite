Hello!

I'm Joseph, a graphic designer and writer working in the Pacific Northwest. In 2018, I received a BFA in Graphic Design with a minor in Literary Arts and Studies from RISD.

Currently, I am freelancing as I seek full-time employment. As a designer, I've recently worked with LA studio Darkroom and designed a new debit card for Finnest. As a writer, I have blogged for RISD Media Group and tutored at the RISD Center for Arts & Language. When I'm not designing or writing, I like being outside, hanging with dogs, and food is also good.
