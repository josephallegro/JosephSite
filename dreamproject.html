University of Washington Dream Project
Brand Identity Design

Greta Skagerlind and I designed a new logo for the University of Washington Dream Project, an education program within UW’s Center for Experiential Learning and Diversity. The Dream Project supports Seattle-area middle and high schoolers as they navigate intersectionalities and plan for their futures—hence the paths in the logo.

We also designed an abbreviated horizontal version of the logo for tighter spaces, like pencils!
