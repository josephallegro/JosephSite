The Parasite Museum, an identity

A new identity for The Parasite Museum, designed in Rich Rose's Branding class at RISD.

The parasitic counter of the logo's "P" form sometimes takes on a life of its own, rolling independent of its host through various applications. Here it is in a dependent state:

Passenger is an informational publication of the Parasite Museum, each volume dedicated to a particular family or species.

Second brand extension proposal:

During the research phase, it was discovered that parasite extinction is a looming threat. To combat this, an exhibition/fundraiser was conceived of, titled "Citizen Parasite." The goals: reverse parasite demonization and raise money for research.
