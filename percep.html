Perception Management, LLC

Art Direction, Video

"Perception Management, LLC" was made in collaboration with five of my classmates in Design Studio III. The assignment was to somehow respond Adam Curtis's documentary on perception management, Hypernormalisation.

Our team approached the assignment by questioning how the director manages perceptions in his film. We set out to do the same, applying our visuals to the project syllabus in an attempt to imbue the text with new meaning.
