Equally Seeded, an identity

Identity for Equally Seeded, a campaign which seeks to eliminate produce beauty standards. The name of the campaign was coined to say while not all produce may look beautiful on the outside, it is what’s inside that matters.

The logo (center) was designed as a tribute to the name. It at once invokes “=”, a pair of seeds, and the letter S, for “Seeded”!

The “ugly fruits” which flank the logo were designed to be appealing visual markers which further the playfulness of the identity.
