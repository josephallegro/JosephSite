Eleven Lenses, a web experience

Eleven Lenses is a listening platform for Deerhunter’s album Halcyon Digest that engages with visitors auditorily and visually. Play with it here on Github, but note there is no audio for copyright reasons.

Each of the eleven symbols on the site’s homepage links to a song page. There, the song’s symbol serves as a lens through which a place of memory (triggered by the music) can be explored.
